ANIMATIONS
To demo the animations, click the Play button in Unreal.

Walk - W
Sprint - Hold Shift + W
Fire - Click Left Mouse Button
Reload - R
Aim Down Sights - Hold Right Mouse Button
Aim Down Sights Walk - Hold Right Mouse Button + W
Aim Down Sights Fire - Hold Right Mouse Button + Click Left Mouse Button
Raise/Lower/Change Weapon - Mouse Wheel Down
